Welcome to QassimUniversity


Qassim University is a public university in the Al-Qassim Province of Saudi Arabia.


Profile

It was established in 2004 jointly between King Saud University and Imam Muhammad ibn Saud Islamic University, each of which used it as its Qassim campus. Subsequently, its constituent colleges became a part of Qassim University. The main campus of Qassim University covers about eight square kilometers in the heart of the region. Qassim University has over 38 Colleges, offering 17 PhD, 62 master, and 731 Bachelor and diploma programs [1]. The government approved allotments for Qassim University in 2013 was 2,351 million riyals (approx 626.87 million US Dollar) [2]. There are campuses in different cities in Al-Qassim Province. During 2008, enrolment at Qassim University was 40,000 students. Faculty and staff totalled 3,500. During 2016/2017, the admission department stated that there are 7019 male students and 9692 female students was accepted as new students at Qassim University [3]; and the number of Faculty and staff is approx 6000. In 2016, Qassim University was ranked 6 in Saudi Arabia and 1909 in the World Rank according to webometrics.info [4].Qassim University (QU) appeared in the QS Work Ranking for the first time in 2011. The university has applied for QS stars rating which is an extensive quality audit of the services and facilities provided by the university. In 2015, QS ranked Qassim University as 51 among Arab Region Rankings; and in 2016, the rank increase to 46 among Arab Region Rankings
